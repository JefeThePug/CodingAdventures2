Practical Python Code Adventure

Hello out there. It's me — Captain Xarlos of the STS Solara. You were on her crew, right?

The strangest thing has happened. One moment we were stopping the Aurawroughts from breaking Eos out of her cosmic prison, and the next, I find myself in this thick, eerie jungle.

Something's wrong. I can't find the ship. I can't find the crew. I don't even know where — or even when — we are.

If this transmission reached you, the Solara's systems must have found a way to briefly link us across whatever world we've landed in. I don't understand it, but I do know one thing: your Python skills might be key to setting all of this ...whatever this is... right.

Will you help me? I could use a partner. The universe isn't going to fix itself.
