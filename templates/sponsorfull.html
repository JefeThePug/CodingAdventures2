Partnerships and Sponsors

What We Are

Practical Python Code Adventure is a story-driven coding challenge site focused on problem-solving and learning algorithms by doing, backed by support of an educational Discord community.

How Can I Sponsor?

Sponsorships help support development, keep content accessible, and grow the project. However, sponsors do not influence puzzle content, solutions, or difficulty.

If you enjoy this project and want to support fun educational websites like this, there are four different ways you can be a sponsor:

  • Tier 1: Wayfarer Level
      • One Time Donation of $5+
    • Your username is listed as a Wayfarer Level Patron
  • Tier 1+: Pathfinder Level
      • One Time Donation of $20+
    • Your username is listed as a Pathfinder Level Patron
    • Your GitHub profile is linked
  • Tier 2: Explorer Level
      • $84 Yearly Donation
    • Recognition as an Explorer Level Patron
    • Your company logo (up to 80px high × 100px wide) featured on the site and linked to your website
  • Tier 3: Pioneer Level
      • Yearly Donation of $144
    • Recognition as a Pioneer Level Patron
    • Your company logo (up to 120px high × 300px wide) featured on the site and linked to your website
    • Up to 50-word company profile included with your logo

Donations can be sent via my Buy Me A Coffee profile.

Other inquiries can be sent via email.
